Frequently Asked Questions

What is a bounce checker?

SUthrey's fancy bounce checker is a tool designed to calculate optimal setups based on your floor height preferences.

How do I reset my preferences?

Click the "Preferences" button on the main page to open the panel, then click "Reset to Defaults" to wipe your custom ranking rankings.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes! It is completely free and runs entirely in your web browser.
