Ranking Preferences

Each setup is scored based on your preferences. Setups with higher scores are shown first.

  • Positive values favor setups with that property.
  • Negative values penalize setups with that property.
  • Zero ignores the property entirely.

Example:
Stock = +100 strongly prefers setups for the stock launcher.

Note: Preferences affect ranking, not filtering.
